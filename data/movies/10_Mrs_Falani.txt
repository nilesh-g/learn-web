Title:
Mrs Falani

Production:
December 10, 2025

Director:
Manish Kishore, Madhukar Verma

Actors:
Swara Bhaskar

Genres:
Drama

Brief Idea / Storyline:
Mrs. Falani is a unique drama film where Swara Bhasker portrays nine distinct female characters from different states across India. The film presents nine different stories, each highlighting a distinct character with her own narrative arc. The film centers on the struggles of women as they attempt to fulfill their suppressed desires and overcome the barriers erected to prevent them from living life on their own terms. The narrative celebrates womanhood and explores India's rich cultural diversity through the lens of different women's experiences.

Audience Review:
Swara Bhasker's decision to play nine different characters showcases her remarkable versatility and acting prowess. The film has been anticipated for its ambitious scope and its focus on female-centric narratives. Critics have noted that the film's thematic focus on breaking barriers and celebrating women's agency resonates strongly with contemporary audiences seeking meaningful cinema.

Relevant Information:
Mrs. Falani is a production of Three Arrows Productions Pvt. Ltd., produced by Sofia Agarwal, Meghshyam Gupta, Rakesh Dang, and Rakesh Kapoor. The screenplay and dialogues are by Manish Kishore, while Shweta Ruby wrote the film. The production had the backing of the Chhattisgarh Government. The film is noteworthy for its ambitious narrative structure and Swara Bhasker's commitment to portraying diverse characters, making it a significant endeavor in Indian cinema's representation of women's stories.
