BRIEF IDEA / STORYLINE
---

Single Papa follows Gaurav 'GG' Gehlot, a well-meaning but impulsive 30-year-old man who decides to adopt a baby immediately after his divorce. When he finds an abandoned baby in the back of his car, his sudden choice leaves his entire family bewildered, questioning whether he is truly ready for the responsibilities of parenthood. The series blends humour with heartfelt drama, exploring the everyday chaos and warmth of family life. As GG navigates single fatherhood, he must defy his eccentric family and handle the complexities of raising a child while discovering what it truly means to be a parent.
