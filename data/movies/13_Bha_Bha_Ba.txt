Title:
Bha Bha Ba

Production:
December 18, 2025

Director:
Dhananjay Shankar

Actors:
Dileep, Vineeth Sreenivasan, Dhyan Sreenivasan, Sandy, Balu Varghese, Baiju Santhosh, Saranya Ponvannan, Siddharth Bharathan, Redin Kingsley, with Mohanlal in a cameo

Genres:
Action, Comedy

Brief Idea / Storyline:
Bha Bha Ba (short for "Bhayam, Bhakthi, Bahumanam" meaning Fear, Devotion, Respect) is a Malayalam action-comedy that follows a new Chief Minister of Kerala, C.K. Joseph, who is kidnapped by a mysterious figure known as "The Commoner." A special investigation team is formed under Commissioner Dev G., assisted by ACP Ashkar Ahammed and Joseph's son, Noble, to locate him. The Commoner and his accomplice take Joseph to their hideout where they urge the public to submit letters outlining their grievances, highlighting that politicians only listen during elections. The film blends action, humor, family drama, and social commentary while featuring multiple talented actors in pivotal roles.

Audience Review:
The film has received mixed to negative reviews from critics and audiences. However, critics have praised the performances of the ensemble cast, particularly the appearance of Mohanlal, the first half of the film, the music, direction, cinematography, and the production quality. Some criticism has been directed towards the plot, overuse of references, and forced comedy elements, but the film has gained appreciation for its ambitious scale and star power.

Relevant Information:
Bha Bha Ba marks the directorial debut of Dhananjay Shankar. The film is written by Fahim Safar and Noorin Shereef, with screenplay and additional dialogues by Dhananjay Shankar himself. It is produced by Gokulam Gopalan under Sree Gokulam Movies. The music is composed by Shaan Rahman, cinematography by Armo, and editing by Ranjan Abraham. The film features an ensemble cast with Mohanlal in an extended cameo that has been highlighted in promotions.
