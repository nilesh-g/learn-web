Title:
Retta Thala

Production:
December 25, 2025

Director:
Kris Thirukumaran

Actors:
Arun Vijay (dual role), Siddhi Idnani, Tanya Ravichandran, Yogi Samy, John Vijay, Hareesh Peradi, Balaji Murugadoss

Genres:
Action, Thriller

Brief Idea / Storyline:
Retta Thala (meaning "Two-headed") is a Tamil action thriller in which Arun Vijay plays a powerful dual role. The narrative is structured as a revenge story featuring two distinct characters with different shades - one representing the protagonist and the other, a negative persona. The film explores the battle between two identities as Arun Vijay faces off against himself, creating a unique dynamic within the thriller narrative. The storyline is centered around complex character motivations and personal vendettas, with the dual role allowing for an exploration of contrasting personality traits. The film features high-octane action sequences, dramatic confrontations, and gripping moments of suspense.

Audience Review:
The film has been generally well-received by audiences who appreciated the intensity and action sequences. Arun Vijay's dual role performance has been praised for bringing depth and versatility to the film. The trailer generated significant buzz with its promise of raw intensity, high-octane action, and dramatic elements. The film's music composed by Sam CS has been highlighted as one of the strengths, adding to the overall cinematic experience. Audiences have responded positively to the revenge narrative and the physical demands of Arun Vijay's dual performance.

Relevant Information:
Retta Thala is written and directed by Kris Thirukumaran, marking his third directorial venture after successful films like "Maan Karate" (2014) and "Gethu" (2016). The film is produced by Bobby Balachandran under the BTG Universal banner. The music is scored by Sam CS, with cinematography by Tijo Tomy and editing by Anthony. The film also marks the Tamil debut of Kannada actor Yogi Samy, who portrays an antagonist. The runtime is crisp at 1 hour and 53 minutes, and the film has been certified U/A. The release on December 25, 2025, positions it among the Christmas releases alongside multiple other films across different languages.
