Brief Idea / Storyline:
Black Rose is a thriller centered around the mysterious death of a young thief named Titli who dies in what appears to be a simple road accident. The case seems straightforward to the police, but Titli's mother insists her daughter could neither drive nor steal, claiming the death is not accidental. This prompts authorities to hire a Special Investigating Officer, Tara, who begins to unravel a massive conspiracy hidden beneath the surface. As she digs deeper, she discovers a concealed conspiracy known as "Black Rose" that connects to much larger sinister forces.

Audience Review:
The film has received praise for its gripping suspenseful narrative and the strong performance of Samiksha Bhatnagar as ACP Tara. Critics have highlighted the film's raw portrayal of police work, including the pressures faced by officers and political interference. Jaishankar Tripathi has been noted for his brilliant supporting performance as a police inspector. The suspenseful plot with unexpected twists has been appreciated by audiences seeking thrillers.
