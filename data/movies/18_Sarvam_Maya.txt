Brief Idea / Storyline:
Sarvam Maya (Everything is a Delusion) is a Malayalam horror-comedy fantasy film that marks the return of Nivin Pauly to his beloved charming persona. The film follows Prabhendu, an aspiring guitarist who distances himself from his affluent Nampoothiri household to pursue music. Circumstances force him to return home where he and his cousin, played by Aju Varghese, exploit his knowledge of Vedas and rituals to make quick money. During an exorcism ritual, Prabhendu encounters a ghostâ€”not the typical bloodthirsty kind found in Malayalam cinema, but a gentle spirit still trying to make sense of her existence. Named "Delulu" (meaning illusion), the ghost becomes a focal point of the narrative as Prabha and his cousin work to uncover her identity and help her attain salvation. The film blends these supernatural elements with comedy, romance, and emotional depth.

Audience Review:
Sarvam Maya has been generally well-received by audiences and critics, particularly praised for its heartwarming and feel-good nature. The film has been described as a triumphant return for Nivin Pauly, marking his commercial comeback after a series of unsuccessful films. Critics have appreciated the direction, cinematography, script, music, and the light-hearted theme. The chemistry between Nivin Pauly and Riya Shibu has been highlighted as a strength, with Riya Shibu's performance described as irresistible and charming.

Relevant Information:
Sarvam Maya is produced by Dr. Ajayya Kumar and Rajeev Menon under Firefly Films. The film is written and directed by Akhil Sathyan, marking his second directorial venture. The cinematography is handled by Sharan Velayudhan, with music composed by Justin Prabhakaran. The film released on December 25, 2025 as a Christmas release, positioning it as a festive family-friendly entertainer perfect for holiday viewing.
