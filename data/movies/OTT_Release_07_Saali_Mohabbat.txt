BRIEF IDEA / STORYLINE
---

Saali Mohabbat is a psychological crime thriller centered on Smita, a small-town housewife whose peaceful life is shattered when her husband and cousin are found murdered in her own house. When everyone believes Smita is the culprit, she becomes entangled in a complex investigation led by Inspector Ratan. The narrative unfolds as a cat-and-mouse chase between the suspect and the investigator, exploring themes of marriage, secrets, lies, revenge, and betrayal. Inspired by an actual case, the film delves into the dark side of relationships and the hidden lives people lead behind closed doors.

---
AUDIENCE REVIEW
---

Saali Mohabbat has received critical acclaim for its gripping narrative and powerful performances. Radhika Apte's portrayal of Smita has been particularly praised, with viewers noting her ability to convey fear, anger, pain, and hidden power through her eyes. Divyendu Sharma has been lauded for his transformation from his well-known comedic roles, delivering a nuanced performance. The film's atmospheric cinematography and slow-burn mystery have resonated with audiences who appreciate psychological thrillers. Critics have noted it as a refreshing entry in the thriller genre that relies on character development and suspense rather than action.
