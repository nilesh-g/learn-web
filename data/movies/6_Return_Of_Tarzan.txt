Title:
Return Of Tarzan

Production:
December 6, 2025

Director:
B. Shekhar

Actors:
Hemant Birje, Nidhi Sharma

Genres:
Action, Drama

Brief Idea / Storyline:
Return of Tarzan is an action-drama film that brings back the classic Tarzan narrative within the Hindi film industry. The story follows the jungle protagonist through various adventures and challenges, maintaining the spirit of adventure and survival that the character is known for.

Audience Review:
The film aims to appeal to audiences who enjoy action-adventure narratives. Hemant Birje takes on the iconic role with support from co-star Nidhi Sharma, offering a fresh take on a classic character.

Relevant Information:
The film's music is composed by Sharafat Khan. It belongs to the action and drama genres, providing audiences with an entertaining mix of adventure and dramatic storytelling. The film represents an attempt to adapt the Tarzan legacy for contemporary Hindi cinema audiences.
