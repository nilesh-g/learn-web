Title:
Kirik Party Remake

Production:
December 1, 2025

Director:
Abhishek Jain

Actors:
Kartik Aaryan

Genres:
Comedy, Romance

Brief Idea / Storyline:
The Hindi remake of the Kannada film "Kirik Party" follows a first-year engineering student who joins college and forms friendships with his hostel mates. The story revolves around college life, youthful relationships, and the bonds formed between students as they navigate their college years with humor and emotional depth.

Audience Review:
The original Kannada version was well-received for its fresh take on college life and its relatable characters. The Hindi remake aims to bring this engaging narrative to North Indian audiences with Kartik Aaryan's charismatic presence leading the film.

Relevant Information:
The original Kirik Party, released in 2016, was directed by Rishab Shetty and marked the acting debut of Rashmika Mandanna. This remake seeks to adapt the beloved story for Hindi cinema while maintaining the essence of college camaraderie and youthful adventures that made the original successful.
