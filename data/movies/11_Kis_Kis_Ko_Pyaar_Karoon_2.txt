Title:
Kis Kis Ko Pyaar Karoon 2

Production:
December 12, 2025

Director:
Anukalp Goswami

Actors:
Kapil Sharma, Manjot Singh, Hira Warina, Tridha Choudhury, Parul Gulati, Ayesha Khan, Asrani, Akhilendra Mishra

Genres:
Comedy, Romance

Brief Idea / Storyline:
Kis Kis Ko Pyaar Karoon 2 is a comedy film that revolves around Mohan Sharma, played by Kapil Sharma, who gets married to multiple women in a series of comedic circumstances. The protagonist is a man with three wives, and as he juggles between them with different identities (Mohan, Mehboob/Michael), the film progresses through hilarious situations of confusion and deception. The narrative involves a love interest named Sania, conversions, unexpected marriages, and his eventual encounters with the law when he becomes entangled with a police inspector.

Audience Review:
The film is positioned as a lighthearted comedy with unlimited fun, delivering triple the romance and confusion. Kapil Sharma's comedic timing and the ensemble cast's chemistry promise entertainment for audiences seeking humorous cinema. The film features a vibrant cast with good comic timing and engaging performances.

Relevant Information:
This is the second installment in the "Kis Kis Ko Pyaar Karoon" franchise. The film is produced by Ratan Jain, Ganesh Jain & Abbas Mustan. It stars Kapil Sharma in the lead role and features music by Yo Yo Honey Singh, with the album title track being a remake of a classic Bollywood song. The film has been designed as a family-friendly comedy entertaining audiences during the festive season.
