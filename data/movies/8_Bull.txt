Title:
Bull

Production:
December 8, 2025

Director:
Aditya Nimbalkar (also noted as Sangeeth Sivan in some sources)

Actors:
Shahid Kapoor

Genres:
Drama, Action

Brief Idea / Storyline:
Bull is an action film set in the 1980s and inspired by real-life events from the life of Brigadier Bulsara of the Indian Army. The film follows a paratrooper who leads his men through a historic and selfless military mission that has become iconic for its precision and bravado. The narrative explores themes of heroism, sacrifice, and patriotism during a significant period in military history.

Audience Review:
The film has generated considerable anticipation due to Shahid Kapoor's commitment to action-heavy roles. The casting of one of Bollywood's finest actors in a role based on a real military figure has elevated expectations for the film's authenticity and dramatic depth.

Relevant Information:
Bull is produced by Bhushan Kumar and Krishan Kumar of T-Series, along with Guilty By Association partners Amar Butala and Garima Mehta. The story and screenplay are by Aseem Arrora and Parveez Shaikh. This is the second collaboration between Shahid Kapoor and Bhushan Kumar, following the success of "Kabir Singh" in 2019. The film represents a significant production with a grand scale action narrative.
