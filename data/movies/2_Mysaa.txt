Title:
Mysaa

Production:
December 1, 2025

Director:
Rawindra Pulle

Actors:
Rashmika Mandanna, Eshwari Rao, Guru Somasundaram, Rao Ramesh

Genres:
Action, Thriller

Brief Idea / Storyline:
Mysaa is a high-octane emotional action thriller that ventures deep into the culturally rich and captivating world of the Gond tribes. Rashmika Mandanna portrays a Gond woman marked by trauma and rage. The story reveals a woman shaped by pain and suffering, whose anger is strong enough to shake nature itself. The first glimpse shows her walking towards her enemies without hesitation, with a handcuff dangling from her hand and a gun in her grip, suggesting a past filled with struggle and conflict.

Audience Review:
The first glimpse generated significant buzz with fans praising Rashmika Mandanna's fierce and transformative avatar. Her portrayal of a formidable Gond woman showcases strength, ferocity, and emotional gravity, marking a departure from her previous roles. The cinematography by Shreyaas P Krishna and the background score by Jakes Bejoy have been particularly appreciated. Action choreography by Andy Long promises high-quality fight sequences.

Relevant Information:
Mysaa is Rawindra Pulle's directorial debut. The film is produced by Ajay-Anil Sayyapureddy and Veerasai Gopa under Unformula Films, with co-production by Srikanth Reddy Sathi. The film is being shot in Telangana and the dense forests of Kerala. The music label is T-Series. This marks Rashmika Mandanna's venture into a completely new territory as an action heroine.
