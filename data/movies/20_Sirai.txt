Title:
Sirai

Production:
December 25, 2025

Director:
Suresh Rajakumari

Actors:
Vikram Prabhu, Anishma Anilkumar, LK Akshay Kumar, Ananda Thambirajah

Genres:
Thriller

Brief Idea / Storyline:
Sirai is a Tamil thriller that is based on a real-life incident. The film tells the story of a police officer who is tasked with transporting a murder suspect from Vellore jail to Sivagangai court. The journey becomes a complex exploration of their difficult interactions, the officer's insights into the case, and the accused's complicated past. The film examines the dynamics between the law enforcer and the accused, revealing layers of their respective situations and the moral complexities involved. As the journey unfolds, unexpected turns occur that challenge perceptions and create tension throughout the narrative.

Audience Review:
The film is positioned as a gripping thriller that examines real-life incidents through a cinematic lens. With a strong cast including Vikram Prabhu and LK Akshay Kumar, the film aims to deliver suspense and drama rooted in authentic scenarios.

Relevant Information:
Sirai is directed by Suresh Rajakumari and features Vikram Prabhu as the central character. The film was released on December 25, 2025, as a Christmas release in Tamil cinema. The narrative structure of a tense journey from one location to another provides the framework for exploring deeper themes of justice, morality, and human connections.
