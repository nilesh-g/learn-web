Title:
Mindiyum Paranjum

Production:
December 25, 2025

Director:
Arun Bose

Actors:
Unni Mukundan, Aparna Balamurali

Genres:
Drama

Brief Idea / Storyline:
Mindiyum Paranjum is a Malayalam drama set in the picturesque, rainy landscapes of Idukki. The film focuses on a mature love story between a married couple named Sanal and Leena as they explore their relationship over multiple life phases. The narrative emphasizes their journey through memories, longing, and finding peace. The film highlights the subtleties of communication—or lack thereof—in partnerships, exploring the complexities of long-term relationships with intimacy and authenticity. Set against the backdrop of Kerala's natural beauty, the film uses the rain-soaked landscapes of Idukki as more than just scenery, making it integral to the storytelling and emotional atmosphere.

Audience Review:
The film is positioned as a drama that explores the depths of mature relationships. With lead performances from Unni Mukundan and Aparna Balamurali, the film aims to present an authentic look at marriage and partnership in contemporary Kerala society.

Relevant Information:
Mindiyum Paranjum is directed by Arun Bose and features Unni Mukundan and Aparna Balamurali in lead roles. The film was released on December 25, 2025, aligning with the Christmas release schedule that saw multiple Malayalam films competing for audience attention during the festive season.
