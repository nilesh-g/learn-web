BRIEF IDEA / STORYLINE
---

Bhay - The Gaurav Tiwari Mystery is an investigative supernatural thriller inspired by the life and mysterious death of Gaurav Tiwari, India's first paranormal investigator. The series explores his paranormal legacy while investigating his mysterious death in 2016. As journalist and author Irene Venkat delves into Tiwari's life and work, she uncovers the truth behind his untimely death at age 32 and discovers a deeper, unsolved mystery that blurs the line between reality and the supernatural. The narrative incorporates his real-life case files and documented paranormal investigations while exploring the psychological and emotional toll of investigating the unknown.

---
AUDIENCE REVIEW
---

The series has been praised for its intriguing premise and the stellar performances of its lead actors. Karan Tacker's portrayal of Gaurav Tiwari has been noted as terrific and emotionally nuanced, capturing the character's dedication to paranormal investigation. Kalki Koechlin's performance as Irene Venkat has been appreciated for balancing professional curiosity with emotional vulnerability. Audiences have found the blend of real-life case files with fictional elements to create a gripping narrative that keeps them invested throughout the series.

---
RELEVANT INFORMATION
---

Bhay - The Gaurav Tiwari Mystery premiered on Amazon MX Player on December 12, 2025, as a 6-episode series. It is inspired by Abhirup Dhar's book 'Ghost Hunter Gaurav Tiwari: The Life and Legacy of India's Foremost Paranormal Investigator' (2021). The series was directed by Robbie Grewal and written by Arshad Syed, produced by Prabhleen Sandhu under Almighty Motion Picture. Principal photography took place between October 2024 and April 2025.
