BRIEF IDEA / STORYLINE
---

Real Kashmir Football Club is an inspirational sports drama based on true events, detailing the compelling story of two men who established Kashmir's first professional soccer club. The series explores the untold journey of a team that rose against the odds in conflict-hit Jammu and Kashmir. It showcases the story of how RKFC transformed football in the Valley and inspired a generation of young players to dream beyond violence and despair. From assembling players to managing coaches and securing funding, the narrative captures the challenges and triumphs of building a football club in a region marked by conflict.
