BRIEF IDEA / STORYLINE
---

Ek Deewane Ki Deewaniyat is a romantic thriller that explores the dark side of obsessive love. The story revolves around Vikramaditya Bhonsle, a powerful and influential politician who falls head over heels for Adaa Randhawa, a glamorous and free-spirited film actress. What begins as a sweet and passionate romance between two people from different worlds quickly transforms into something darker and more dangerous. Vikramaditya's feelings of love gradually morph into an all-consuming obsession as he becomes increasingly fixated on possessing Adaa 'by hook or by crook.' His insecurities, ego, and need for control begin to shadow every moment they share. As the narrative unfolds, the audience witnesses how a beautiful love story can turn toxic when passion is mixed with possession. The film delves deep into themes of control, jealousy, and the psychological breakdown that occurs when love becomes obsession, leading to an inevitable clash between power, fear, and unchecked desire.

---
AUDIENCE REVIEW
---

Ek Deewane Ki Deewaniyat has generated significant discussion among audiences for its unflinching portrayal of obsessive love. Harshvardhan Rane's performance has been particularly acclaimed, with viewers praising his ability to convey brooding intensity and emotional vulnerability as he descends into obsession. Sonam Bajwa has received praise for lending grace and quiet strength to her character, balancing glamour with restraint. The film's chemistry between the leads is noted as compelling, though occasionally uneven. Audiences have appreciated the film's refusal to sanitize its theme, presenting a dark, realistic picture of how love can become dangerous. However, some viewers have found the first half to be stretched and predictable. The visual polish of the film, steeped in nostalgia and Mumbai's vibrant backdrop, has been highlighted as a positive element.

---
RELEVANT INFORMATION
---

Ek Deewane Ki Deewaniyat premiered on Netflix on December 16, 2025, following its theatrical release on October 21, 2025. The film was directed by Milap Zaveri and produced under the banner Desi Movies Factory. The screenplay explores how societal power dynamics play into romantic relationships and the potential for abuse of power when love becomes obsession. The film is rated 15+ due to its mature themes of obsession and emotional abuse. With a runtime of approximately 2 hours, it offers a concentrated examination of toxic relationship dynamics in contemporary India.
