Title:
Champion

Production:
December 25, 2025

Director:
Pradeep Advaitham

Actors:
Roshan Meka, Anaswara Rajan, Kay Kay Menon, Santhosh Pratap, Kovai Sarala, Vennela Kishore, Prakash Raj, Dulquer Salmaan

Genres:
Drama, Sports

Brief Idea / Storyline:
Champion is a period sports drama that follows a young man named Michael who travels to London with dreams of becoming a professional football player. The narrative explores his journey as he pursues his ambition in a foreign land, navigating challenges, cultural differences, and personal conflicts. As circumstances take unexpected turns, Michael faces obstacles that test his determination and character. The film examines themes of perseverance, identity, ambition, and the complexities of following one's dreams despite adversities. Through Michael's story, the film provides insights into the struggles of pursuing excellence and the personal sacrifices involved in achieving one's goals. The presence of prominent actors like Kay Kay Menon, Prakash Raj, and Dulquer Salmaan in supporting roles adds depth to the narrative.

Audience Review:
Champion is positioned as an ambitious sports drama that marks the Telugu debut of Malayalam actress Anaswara Rajan. The film has been described as a significant production backed by the makers of acclaimed films like "Kalki 2898 AD," "Mahanti," "Sita Ramam," and "Jathi Ratnalu." Anaswara Rajan has spoken about the challenges of learning Telugu for the film, highlighting her commitment to the role. The ensemble cast, particularly Roshan Meka's lead performance as Michael, has been anticipated as the film's driving force. The production quality and cinematic scale have been noted as impressive aspects of the film.

Relevant Information:
Champion is written, directed, and has dialogues by Pradeep Advaitham and is produced by Priyanka Dutt, GK Mohan, and Gemini Kiran under the banners Swapna Cinema, Zee Studios, and Anandi Art Creations. The cinematography is handled by R. Madhie, with editing by Kotagiri Venkateswara Rao. The music is composed by Mickey J. Meyer. The film features production design by Thota Tharani and stunt choreography by Peter Hein. The runtime is 1 hour and 39 minutes (99 minutes). Champion was released on December 25, 2025, as a theatrical release in Telugu cinema, marking an important release during the festive season.
