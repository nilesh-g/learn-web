Title:
3 Days 4 Nights In Bihar

Production:
December 3, 2025

Director:
Abhishek Dogra

Actors:
Sharman Joshi, Ruslaan Mumtaz, Jackky Bhagnani

Genres:
Comedy

Brief Idea / Storyline:
The film follows the story of three spoilt, wayward, and rich young men who embark on a nightmarish vacation through Bihar. Their ill-fated trip becomes a comedic adventure as they navigate the unpredictable situations and cultural landscape of Bihar. The story is described as blending the charm and camaraderie of "Dil Chahta Hai" with the socially conscious elements of "Rang De Basanti," set against the backdrop of Bihar.

Audience Review:
The film is positioned as a comedy-drama that combines humor with adventure. Sharman Joshi, Ruslaan Mumtaz, and Jackky Bhagnani's chemistry and comedic timing are expected to be the highlight of the film, with the trio's escapades in Bihar providing ample entertainment value.

Relevant Information:
The original production intended to feature Salman Khan in a lead role, but he suggested a younger actor for the part, leading to Ruslaan Mumtaz being cast instead. Director Abhishek Dogra modified the script accordingly. The film promises a fresh take on the buddy-comedy genre with the unique setting of Bihar adding distinctive cultural flavor to the narrative.
