Title:
Tu Meri Main Tera Main Tera Tu Meri

Production:
December 25, 2025

Director:
Sameer Vidwans

Actors:
Kartik Aaryan, Ananya Panday, Neena Gupta, Jackie Shroff, Tiku Talsania

Genres:
Romance, Comedy, Drama

Brief Idea / Storyline:
Tu Meri Main Tera Main Tera Tu Meri (meaning "You're mine, I'm yours, I'm yours, you're mine") is a romantic comedy film that tells the story of Rehaan "Ray" Mehra, a cynical wedding planner based in Los Angeles who runs the agency Hitched Forever with his independent single mother, Pinky. During a luxury yacht cruise in Croatia, Ray meets Rumi Vardhan, an aspiring novelist from Agra who is on a solo trip before her sister's wedding. As their paths cross, a journey of love unfolds against picturesque international and Indian backdrops including Croatia, Rajasthan, and Agra. The film explores themes of love, connection, and modern relationships in the contemporary world.

Audience Review:
The film has received mixed to negative reviews from critics, though there is praise for the cinematography and production values. The chemistry between Kartik Aaryan and Ananya Panday has been highlighted, and Kartik's performance has been noted as engaging. The breathtaking locations and catchy tracks have been appreciated. However, criticisms have been directed towards the story, screenplay, and direction. Despite the mixed reviews, the film has been positioned as a family-friendly romantic comedy suitable for holiday viewing.

Relevant Information:
Tu Meri Main Tera Main Tera Tu Meri is a Dharma Productions and Namah Pictures production, marking a reconciliation between Kartik Aaryan and Karan Johar after their public dispute in 2021. The film was written by Karan Shrikant Sharma and directed by Sameer Vidwans. The music is composed by Vishal and Sheykhar, with cinematography by Anil Mehta. The title track is a recreation of the classic Bollywood song "Saat Samundar Paar." Principal photography was completed in 57 days across locations in Croatia, Nawalgarh, Agra, and Mumbai.
