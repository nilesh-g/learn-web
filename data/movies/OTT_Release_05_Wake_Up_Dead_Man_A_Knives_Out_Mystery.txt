BRIEF IDEA / STORYLINE
---

Wake Up Dead Man: A Knives Out Mystery is the third and darkest installment of Rian Johnson's acclaimed murder mystery franchise. Detective Benoit Blanc returns for his most dangerous case yet, teaming up with an earnest young priest to investigate a perfectly impossible crime at a small-town church with a dark history. When a monsignor is found murdered under mysterious circumstances, Blanc must navigate the complex web of secrets, lies, and hidden motives within the church congregation. The film explores themes of resurrection, guidance, and faith while maintaining the franchise's signature blend of clever plotting and character-driven storytelling.

---
AUDIENCE REVIEW
---

The film has been praised for its intricate plot, stellar ensemble cast, and Rian Johnson's masterful direction. Critics and audiences appreciate Daniel Craig's return as the charming detective Benoit Blanc and his excellent chemistry with new cast members. The mystery itself has been lauded for its complexity and clever twists that keep viewers engaged until the final revelation. Audiences have noted that while the film takes a darker tone compared to its predecessors, it maintains the franchise's wit and clever dialogue.

---
RELEVANT INFORMATION
---

Wake Up Dead Man: A Knives Out Mystery premiered in select theaters on November 26, 2025, before arriving on Netflix on December 12, 2025. The film is a Netflix original and is available exclusively on the platform. It stars a powerhouse ensemble cast and boasts a runtime of approximately 2 hours and 15 minutes. The film's title is inspired by a 1997 U2 song, connecting to the church theme and the film's exploration of faith and redemption.
