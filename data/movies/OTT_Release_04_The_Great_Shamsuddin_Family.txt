BRIEF IDEA / STORYLINE
---

The Great Shamsuddin Family is a dramedy centered on Bani Ahmed, who has just 12 hours to finish an important application for an international career opportunity when her entire family unexpectedly shows up at her apartment, plunging her into a chaotic crisis. Set within a single day in Delhi, the storyline follows Bani as her mothers, aunts, cousins, and past love interests converge, each bringing their own crises and complications. While navigating through interfaith issues, generational clashes, and familial obligations, Bani must decide between chasing her international career prospects or staying connected with her family. The film explores the contemporary Indian family dynamics and the often messy but ultimately loving nature of family relationships.

AUDIENCE REVIEW
---

The series has been well-received by audiences who appreciate its humorous yet heartfelt approach to family dynamics. Viewers have praised the ensemble cast for their strong performances and the natural chemistry between the characters. The film's clever writing and relatable conflicts have resonated with audiences who see reflections of their own families on screen. Critics and viewers alike have commended director Anusha Rizvi's return to feature filmmaking after 14 years, noting that the film successfully blends comedy with emotional honesty.

RELEVANT INFORMATION
---

The Great Shamsuddin Family premiered on JioHotstar on December 12, 2025, marking filmmaker Anusha Rizvi's return to feature filmmaking after her acclaimed debut Peepli Live (2010). The film is produced by Ajit Andhare, Alok Jain, and Mahmood Farooqui under Star Studios 18 and Third World Films. The music is composed by Simran Hora with lyrics by the late Ghulam Rabbani Taban. The production design is credited to Madhusudhan N, with cinematography by Debashish Remy Dalai.
